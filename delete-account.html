Delete Your Account

App Name: Society Hisab

If you wish to delete your Society Hisab account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to secucorelabs@gmail.com with the following details:

  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • The email address registered with your Society Hisab account
  • Your registered name and flat/house number (for verification)

We will process your request and confirm once your account and data have been deleted, typically within 7 business days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon a verified deletion request, the following data associated with your account will be permanently deleted from our systems:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Your flat/house number and account login credentials
  • Any personal profile information

What Data May Be Retained

Some data may be retained even after account deletion, where necessary for the continued integrity of the society's financial records:

  • Historical financial transactions (income/expense entries) you submitted while acting as an Admin, since these form part of the shared society ledger used by other members. These entries will be anonymized where possible (your name removed, but the transaction record kept for accounting continuity).
  • Information we are legally required to retain for accounting, audit, or compliance purposes.

Important Notes

  • If you are the Admin of a society, please ensure another member is designated as the new Admin before requesting deletion, so the society's account management can continue uninterrupted.
  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
