Privacy Policy

App Name: Society Hisab
Last Updated: 22 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Society Hisab ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to operate the App's core features (society management and expense tracking):

Data Type Purpose
Google Account Information (name, email address) Used for account creation, login, and verification (Google Sign-In / OTP verification)
Phone Number Used for account/identity verification (OTP)
Society Details (society name, address, society code) Created by the Admin to set up and identify a society within the App
Member Information When a member joins a society using a society code shared by the Admin
Financial / Ledger Data (expense amounts, contribution amounts, balances) Manually entered by the Admin to maintain society accounts and display dashboard totals
Photos (bill/receipt images) Uploaded by the Admin via Camera or Gallery as proof of expenses
Device Permissions (Camera, Storage) Required only to capture or upload bill/receipt photos

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and verify user accounts (via Google Sign-In and OTP).
  • To allow an Admin to create a society profile and generate a unique society code.
  • To allow members to join an existing society using that society code.
  • To record, store, and display society income, expenses, and balance on the dashboard.
  • To allow Admins to upload bill/receipt photos as supporting proof for expenses.
  • To maintain and improve the functioning and reliability of the App.

3. Data Storage and Security

All society, member, and financial data is securely transmitted to and stored on our backend server/API. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Information entered by an Admin (such as society balance, expenses, and bill photos) is visible only to members who have joined that specific society using the valid society code.

We do not share your data with advertisers or external marketing companies.

5. Camera and Storage Permissions

The App requests Camera and Storage/Photo permissions solely to allow Admins to capture or select bill/receipt images for expense records. These permissions are not used for any other purpose, and images are not accessed without explicit user action.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for use by adults managing residential society/community accounts. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information for as long as your account or society remains active on the App. If you wish to delete your account or request removal of your data, please contact us using the details below.

8. Your Rights

  • You may request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • You may request correction or deletion of your data.
  • You may withdraw consent by discontinuing use of the App and contacting us to delete your account.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
